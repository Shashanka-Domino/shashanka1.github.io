Our Story

Jesus

Jesus is originally from the south side of Chicago. He is a graduate of the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign where he also met his college sweetheart and new fiance, Cherita! Jesus, commonly referred to as Zeus, enjoys the three B's: Books, Beer, and Burgers. He enjoys exploring the city for all three things with his love by his side. Jesus is now ready to write the next chapter of his book as he marries his college sweetheart, Cherry!

She loves visiting with her family in her hometown of Naperville. After she met Jesus her freshman year in college, she realized that she loved his sense of adventure and patience the most.
